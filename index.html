Experience

GU Orbit

September 2022 - Present

I work as Software Engineer at GU Orbit a student-led society at the University of Glasgow, with an aim to become the first student society to launch a satellite into space. As part of the Cloudview Balloon Launch mission, worked on the Python scripts and interfaced BMP180 sensors to Rasberry Pi with i2c connection. Right now I am developing the prototype of the processing pipeline to be deployed on a aerial testing platform. We are also in early stages of the Astraeus-01 satellite mission, which will be a 4 year Low Earth Orbit CubeSat mission, this mission will briefly consist of sending this small satellite into a Sun Synchronous Orbit.
